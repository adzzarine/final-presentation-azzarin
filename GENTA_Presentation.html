05
Risk Management
What can go wrong — and how we prevent it
Every risk identified, probability assessed, mitigation designed. Nothing is left unaddressed.
High priority risks
GENTA gives wrong answer — legal/compliance impact
Mitigation: RAG grounded only on verified Paragon docs. Confidence threshold — low confidence forces escalation. Quarterly KB audit by HRS. All answers cite source document.
High
Data privacy breach — employee personal data exposed
Mitigation: Read-only Sunfish access, scoped per user. No PII stored in analytics pipeline. DPO review required before each phase. UU PDP compliance built in from Phase 0.
High
Low adoption — employees don't trust or use GENTA
Mitigation: Soft launch with 50 pilot users, gather feedback first. Embed in existing WhatsApp workflow — no new app required. Clear "GENTA is not replacing anyone" comms.
Med
Medium & operational risks
Sunfish integration instability
Mitigation: Phase 0 runs without Sunfish. Read-only first, stress-tested before write. Fallback: KB-only mode if API down. SLA defined with IT team.
Med
HRS team overwhelmed by GENTA escalations
Mitigation: Escalation routing to correct PIC — not a general queue. KB gap tracker — high-escalation topics get KB update within 5 days. Escalation SLA defined per topic.
Med
KB becomes stale — answers drift from policy
Mitigation: Policy change → KB update within 48h. HRS owns KB editor (no IT needed). Quarterly full KB review. GENTA flags "last updated X months ago" in answer.
Low
Scope creep — GENTA tries to do too much too soon
Mitigation: Phase gate reviews before each capability unlock. Write access only after Phase 0 trust metrics met. Human override always available.
Low
Governance principle: Every GENTA action is logged, reviewable, and reversible within 24 hours. HRS always has override control. Trust is built incrementally — no capability is granted without proven track record.
06
Communication Plan
Introducing GENTA to 16,000+ people
A phased comms approach — from internal HRS alignment to full workforce rollout. Trust is built through transparency, not surprises.
comms phases
Month 1–2 — Internal Alignment
HRS team trained. GENTA introduced to BGP, TA, FM, Rewards cross-function. Get buy-in from key HR stakeholders before employee launch.
Month 2–3 — Pilot (50 users)
Soft launch with volunteer early adopters from HO. Gather qualitative feedback. Fix KB gaps. Measure resolution rate before scaling.
Month 3 — HO Rollout
Official launch to all HO employees. Town hall format — "GENTA is here to help, not replace." Demo live. First 30 days: weekly GENTA feedback pulse.
Month 4 — Plant & DC Rollout
WhatsApp-first for Plant and DC (where Paraportal access is lower). HR Plant champion per location. Non-employee onboarding to GENTA via WA.
Ongoing — Nurture & Expand
Monthly "GENTA learns" newsletter. Feature highlights when new capability launches. Feedback loop always open.
key messages per audience
To employees (all workforce)

"GENTA handles the FAQ volume so you can focus on what only humans can do — judgment, empathy, and strategy. You are the architect; GENTA is the executor."

What NOT to say: Never frame GENTA as "replacing HR staff." Never claim GENTA is always right. Always position as "HR-backed, always escalates when uncertain."
